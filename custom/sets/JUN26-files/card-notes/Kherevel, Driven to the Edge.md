506.3? One card, Kherevel, Driven to the Edge, states that if a creature attacks, it attacks each player and permanent it could attack. In this case, if a creature attacks, the attacking player chooses for each permanent or player whether or not to pay any costs associated with attacking that permanent or player. Then, the creature attacks all permanents and players with no cost associated with attacking them, and all permanents and players for whom the cost to attack them was paid, simultaneously. For as long as that creature remains an attacking creature, it’s attacking all of them instead of just one. Players can block that creature as normal if it’s attacking them or a permanent they block for.
